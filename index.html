Hwijae Son

Assistant Professor · Department of Mathematics · Konkuk University

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Konkuk University. Prior to this, I served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Software at Hanbat National University and worked as a postdoctoral researcher at the Stochastic Analysis & Application Research Center at KAIST.

I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from POSTECH, under the supervision of Professor Hyung Ju Hwang. My doctoral research focused on Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs). I completed my undergraduate studies in Mathematics at Hanyang University.

My research interests include Physics-Informed Machine Learning, Inverse Problems, Graph Neural Networks, and Recommender Systems.
